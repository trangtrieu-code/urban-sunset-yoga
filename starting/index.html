Urban Sunset Yoga Studio

Breathe deep. Stretch far. Feel human again.

Join a relaxed but committed group of people who meet after work to unwind, build strength, and reconnect with their bodies in the middle of the city. Every session finishes with a slow cool-down and a walk to one of our favorite local cafés.

Urban Sunset Yoga Collective is a free community group supported by Mindful Motion Studio.

I used to finish work totally drained. Now I plan my evenings around these sessions. I get to move, relax, and actually talk to people instead of scrolling on my phone.

— Samira L., software developer / evening yogi since 2022

We meet every weekday at 6:30 AM in Riverfront Park.

  • When: Monday–Friday, 6:30 AM – 7:30 AM
  • Where: Riverfront Park, north lawn (near the old fountain)
  • What to bring: Yoga mat, water bottle, light jacket for cooler morning
  • Cost: Completely free – just show up and say hi
